EightyPlus

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. Overview

EightyPlus ("the App") is an AI-powered financial news briefing app developed by SandClaw. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Key principle: All personal data stays on your device. We do not operate user accounts or collect personal information on our servers.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account system. No name, email, phone number, or password.
  • No reading history. Your news reading activity is never sent to any server.
  • No AI analysis data. All AI processing runs on-device (Gemma 4 E2B), never uploaded.
  • No bookmarks/saved data. Stored locally on your device only (SQLite).
  • No location data. The App does not request location permissions.
  • No contacts, camera, microphone, or other sensitive permissions

3. Data We Collect

PRO subscribers: No advertising data is collected. Ads are completely removed.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

  • Free users see banner ads powered by Google AdMob
  • PRO subscribers see no ads (all ad SDKs are inactive)
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Google Settings → Ads)

EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

  • A consent dialog is shown before any ad data is collected
  • You may withdraw consent at any time via Settings → Privacy & Ads → Manage ad consent
  • Without consent, ads may still appear but will not be personalized

7. Children's Privacy

EightyPlus is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

  • Access & Deletion: All your data is on your device. Uninstall the app to delete everything.
  • Ad consent withdrawal: Settings → Privacy & Ads → Manage ad consent
  • Ad personalization opt-out: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out

11. Financial Disclaimer

EightyPlus provides news briefings and AI-generated analysis for informational purposes only. Nothing in the App constitutes financial, investment, or trading advice. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

12. AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

The App displays AI-generated analysis summaries alongside news articles. These summaries are:

  • Generated by artificial intelligence. Google Gemini and Gemma 4 E2B are used for analysis.
  • NOT original article content. They are independent AI interpretations based on article titles and metadata.
  • NOT investment, financial, or legal advice. See Section 11.
  • Potentially inaccurate or incomplete. AI models may produce errors or hallucinations.

Always refer to the original source article via the provided link for accurate and complete information. The App does not reproduce or redistribute original article content.
