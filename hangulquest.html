HangulQuest

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Overview

HangulQuest ("the App") is a Korean alphabet (Hangul) learning game developed by SandClaw. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Key principle: All learning progress and preferences stay on your device. We do not operate user accounts or collect personal information on our servers.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account system. No name, email, phone number, or password.
  • No learning history on servers. Your game progress and scores never leave your device.
  • No language preference tracking. Your selected learning language is stored locally only.
  • No location data. The App does not request location permissions.
  • No contacts, camera, microphone, or other sensitive permissions.
  • No analytics SDK. We do not use Firebase Analytics or similar tracking tools.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored using Android SharedPreferences on your device only, and is automatically removed when you uninstall the App:

  • Selected native language (e.g. English, Japanese, Thai) for UI translation
  • Game progress (current level, unlocked levels) for Games 1–4
  • Onboarding completion flag
  • Ad frequency counters (for showing ads at appropriate intervals only)

4. Data Collected by Third-Party SDKs

DataPurposeCollected By
Advertising ID (AAID) Personalized ads Google AdMob SDK
Device info (model, OS version) Ad optimization Google AdMob SDK
IP address (transient) Ad delivery & fraud prevention Google AdMob SDK

We ourselves do not receive, store, or process this data. It is handled entirely by Google AdMob under Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays three types of ads from Google AdMob:

  • Interstitial ads: Shown between game levels (approximately every 2–4 completions)
  • Rewarded ads: Optional — shown only when you choose to watch an ad in exchange for an in-game HP reward
  • App Open ads: Occasionally shown when returning to the App

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

EU / EEA / UK Users (GDPR)

  • On first launch, a consent dialog (Google UMP) is shown before any ad data is collected
  • You may grant, refuse, or customize consent
  • If you refuse, only non-personalized ads will be shown
  • Consent is managed by Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK

California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

  • A privacy options dialog is shown for California residents
  • You may exercise your rights under CCPA/CPRA to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information

6. Children's Privacy

HangulQuest is a language learning app suitable for general audiences. It is not directed specifically at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will remove such information.

7. Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data in Android settings
  • Advertising data: Retained by Google AdMob according to Google's data retention policy

8. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (via Android settings or UMP consent dialog)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (EEA/UK residents)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.
